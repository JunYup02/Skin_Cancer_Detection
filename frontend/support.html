Frequently asked

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. Dermalyze is an informational AI tool, not a substitute for a licensed dermatologist. Always seek professional care for any concerning lesion.

Why does the analysis sometimes fail?

The classification step calls a Vertex AI endpoint that has to be deployed and configured by the Dermalyze team. If it isn't connected yet, you'll see a clear notice on the upload screen instead of a broken result.

Is my photo stored?

Your photo is sent directly to the analysis endpoint for classification. It is not saved to your account or displayed to other users.
